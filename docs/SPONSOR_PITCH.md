**A live, branded sports surface for Wisconsin advertisers.**

## The opportunity

Wausau Pilot & Review is where locals look first for news — and nothing in Wisconsin
travels like the Packers. The **Packers Tracker** is the second build in our live-sports
family (the Brewers Tracker is the proof of concept): a live, tabbed stats experience —
featured game with a kickoff countdown, the NFC North race, playoff odds, the full
schedule, team leaders, and a per-game film room — that updates itself directly from the
league's public data, all season long.

**The title sponsorship is open.** This one-pager is the case for putting a Wisconsin
brand on it before Week 1.

## Why it works for a sponsor

- **Dwell time, not a banner glance.** The widget is interactive and self-updating. On
  game day the score, down & distance, and win probability tick in real time — readers
  stay and watch, with your brand on the marquee the whole time.
- **A weekly appointment.** Football's rhythm is seven days of anticipation around every
  game: the countdown, the injury report, the matchup breakdown, the Monday film room.
  That's seven days of impressions per game week, not three hours.
- **Premium, native placement.** Not an ad slot bolted on — your logo is the title sponsor
  of the page, plus a credit on the live game hero. It reads as a partnership, not an
  interruption.
- **Provable ROI.** Cookieless analytics give a clean monthly report: impressions (page
  views) and click-throughs per placement. Your link carries campaign tags so the traffic
  shows up in your own analytics, too.
